Diwali

Diwali, also known as the Festival of Lights, is a major Hindu festival celebrated with great enthusiasm and joy, symbolizing the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. It involves the lighting of diyas (oil lamps), fireworks, exchange of gifts, and sharing of sweets among family and friends.
